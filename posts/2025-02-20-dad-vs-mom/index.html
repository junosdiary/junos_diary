Dad vs. Mom

February 20, 2025 · Juno

Dad and Mom had a quarrel today. I think it started when Mom accused Dad for working too much, even on weekends, and Dad denied it, as usual, of course. I suppose not everything that Dad does on his computer is considered working, but that’s how Mom perceived it anyways.

So for 3 days, Dad had a really long face, pretending there is nothing wrong. When Mom asked “Are you OK?”, it always “Yeah, I am fine”.

I honestly don’t understand what the big deal is. Just cry if you are unhappy, laugh if you are happy. Simple as that!

Life is made up of each individual moment, every unhappy moment takes away from the happy ones. After all, I just need you two to make me happy, as the saying goes, happy Baby, happy life, right ?
