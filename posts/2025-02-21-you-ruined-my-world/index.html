You Ruined My World

February 21, 2025 · Juno

I don’t know how many times you guys have have ruined my world, I’ve lost count, but here they are anyways, because even if you don’t remember them, I do !

18 Months Memories#

  1. Remember those times when I wanted to stay in bath for just two more hours so I can play with my watering can ? For unknown reasons, you just had to drag me out of it, and you keep doing it every day no matter how hard I cried each time !! Ridiculous !!

  2. Mom, remember that time when you bought a Lego car for Declan ? (BTW, he’s not my boyfriend, at least not yet !). I can’t believe that you won’t let me have it. You broke my heart. Fortunately they healed 2 minutes later when I saw a box of nails, then you decided to break my heart again by refusing me. Life was already hard enough !!

  3. Dad, demember that time when we are about to go home after groceries shopping and you insisted on putting me back in the car seat? Why can’t you just let me wander around in the parking lot ? My life was totally ruined after that !!
