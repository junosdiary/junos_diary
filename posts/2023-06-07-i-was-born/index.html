I was born

June 7, 2023 · Juno

This is just a brief introduction to my family. Where I came from, who my mom is, and why I am here

Birth#

I was born at 1:43 AM on 6/7/2023, weighted 7 pounds and 7 oz after my Mom strugged for 24 hours, allegedly anyways. Technically speaking, I am 20 months now, but I like to sound younger, I mean, who doesn’t these days?

At birth, it was supposed to be all sunny and shiny, you know, seeing the lights for the first time and all, but quite the contrary, it was all too dark. Well, again, allegedly, it was because my bilirubin level was too high, whatever that means, so I had to undergo treatment under UV for several days with my eyes covered in total dark.

There goes several days of no sunlight and I had no idea what was happening outsdie, not that I cared anyways. At least I think Dad and Mom were quite worried.

Mother#

My mom was a teacher, but now she’s my full-time personal assistant, without pay. The story I heard was that mom gave up everything she had in China and decided to move to the US with my Dad. Mom’s family disagreed with the relationship and mom fought back fiercely, and maintained the long distance relationship for years. Eventually, they got married, bought a house, and had me - the American dream come true I guess ?

Father#

Dad is some sort of “network engineer”, I guess it must be difficult to connect with people these days that they need an engineer to help networking each other.

I was born networking with two people already, it can’t be that hard, can it ?

Dad always talk to this little screen in front of him, or staring at it for hours, sometimes making noises hitting the board beneth. I don’t think Dad always likes to talking to the screen though, he definitely frown a lot when the word “aidn” is mentioned.

Juno The Name#

It’s rumored that because Mom watched some video (YouTube actually) of this british/korean kid whoes name is also “Juno” and apparently she matches the image of what my mom saw in me. Oh, and of course, I was born in June, which probably contributed to <1% to the final decision. Oh well, but I won’t become what she imagined, I be me, no one else and that is Juno’s promise !

The Diary#

I figured it’s about time that I do something to channel my thoughts into words before I turn 18. You never know someday I might become famous and this would be how I tell folks about my childhood. Or maybe, just maybe, I needed this personal space for things that I can’t tell neither Dad nor Mom.
